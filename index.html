Kanishk Jain

// PhD Candidate · Mila & Université de Montréal

I am a Ph.D. candidate at Mila, co-advised by Prof. Sarath Chandar and Prof. Ross Goroshin. My research interests are continual learning, lifelong learning, and streaming reinforcement learning.

Previously, I completed my Master's at IIIT Hyderabad, co-advised by Prof. Vineet Gandhi and Prof. K. Madhava Krishna, working on visual grounding, language-guided navigation, and multi-view perception.

Email CV Scholar GitHub LinkedIn Twitter
Research
My work centers on continual learning, lifelong learning, and streaming reinforcement learning — how agents can keep learning from non-stationary data without forgetting, and act reliably on streams of experience.
